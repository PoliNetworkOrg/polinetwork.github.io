Hello!

This is an extension of the @PoliRules.

It happened in the past to deal with guys who pretend to be other people, people who make believe a famous person, members who have not set their real name, or simply people who have nothing to do with it and therefore intruders .

Precisely because ours is a network of real and real people, this rule is true, to be real. To some it may seem obvious, useless almost to be clarified, but better to clarify that not to do so.

If an admin doubts your person, believes a fake or you do not have the real name, the admin can ask you to fix the profile. If you do not change what is required, you can consider removing yourself from the group, because we want that in the @PoliNetwork there are only real people.

How does the verification work?

It is at the discretion of the admin, in the limit of common sense. It can be a vowel, a picture, a telegram call, the ways are many.

If you have already verified your "existence" with an admin, and another admin asks you to verify it, you can easily answer that you have already done it with the name of the admin with which you have done it.

Thanks, good continuation in the @PoliNetwork

EXTENSION [CHAT CLEANING] ğŸ§¹

This is an extension of the @PoliRules

The cleaning of the chat is a possible operation on Telegram, because the messages deleted in the supergroups are deleted for all at the same time. It is also carried out on Discord.

Why is cleaning performed?

Imagine if a conversation about cats happened during the summer in the group. Maybe many like them, but a person who is preparing for the entrance test would not want to find certain messages while studying. How to do? The group is kept clean, that's all. However, there are groups to talk about non-school subjects, you can find them all in @PoliExtra. And there, even cats are welcome.

So do not worry if a message (or more) disappears, it can happen. If you think that it should not disappear, do not write it in the group where it disappeared but write privately to the group administrator and talk to each other.

This also applies to any discussions like "why did I get a warn?" or similar, they are all things to be seen privately with the administrator, and if you are sent to the group, they will also be removed.

Thanks, good continuation in the @PoliNetwork
