Questions and answers ðŸ“Œ

The lessons can be followed online, even those in person (the classrooms are set up)
You use webex, you can find all the links to access the personal classrooms of the professors from the personal timetable section of the online services (it works only if you have already filled in the study plan). If you have not completed the study plan, it will be possible (shortly) to access the links by means of a poster / beep. However, the links are so simple that you can build them yourself. https://politecnicomilano.webex.com/meet/profname.surnameprof (yes, it may be slightly different in case of homonymy)
Recording is NOT guaranteed. What is guaranteed is the transmission of the lesson live. Recording is a courtesy of the teacher.

  • Question: But will access to the virtual classroom of the course / professor also be allowed to those who do not have that particular course in the study plan?
    Answer: Each personal classroom can be locked or not, if the teacher leaves it unlocked (by default this is the case) everyone can enter, otherwise he must admit manually. Theoretically speaking, since the limit is 1000 per lesson, teachers should leave it open
  • Question: Where are the links to the recordings?
    Answer: The links to the recordings are not in an easily usable platform. At the end of registration, the professors obtain a link manually and must manually communicate it to the students. They were advised to keep a beep document updated with links to the recordings
  • Question: Where do I download Cisco Webex?
    Answer: From this link https://www.webex.com/downloads.html, download Webex Meetings
  • Question: Is Linux version available?
    Answer: No. There is Cisco Webex Teams VDI for Linux (but it has fewer features and is very unstable). For Linux users, we recommend using the website https://politecnicomilano.webex.com. However, the university said it will report the problem to Cisco
  • Question: How are the teams divided?
    Answer: Person code (the one starting with 10xxxxxx) odd => team 1, even person code => team 2.
    The division is important for any contact-tracing purposes
  • Question: Can I join two Webex lessons at the same time?
    Answer: On Windows yes. The MacOs version seems to give problems on this aspect
