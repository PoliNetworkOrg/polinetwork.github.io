PoliEdro

From 2012 it wants to be a reference point for all students of the Politecnico di Milano, especially for LGBTI + students (Lesbian, Gay, Bisex, Trans, ...). We organize cultural events, such as discussions on current topics and conferences with relevant people on the LGBTI + scene, as well as external events to make groups. From 2017 organizes PoliMi Pride to bring the face of the Politecnico to the Milan Pride.
